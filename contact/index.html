Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Backup & Export ‚Üí Export Data. Your tasks and statistics will be saved as a JSON file.

Can I sync data between devices?

Currently, TaskPomo works offline on each device. You can manually transfer data using the export/import feature.

Is there a premium version?

We're working on a premium version with additional features. Stay tuned!

Which languages are supported?

TaskPomo supports 11 languages: English, Turkish, Spanish, Portuguese, German, French, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Japanese, and Hindi.
