Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2025

Introduction

TaskPomo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application TaskPomo (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. TaskPomo is designed to work entirely offline on your device. All your data, including tasks, timer sessions, and statistics, are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All data created within TaskPomo is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. This includes:

  • Tasks and their completion status
  • Timer session history
  • Statistics and productivity data
  • App settings and preferences

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data from you. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's export feature.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app. This includes:

  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • General usage patterns (screen views, feature usage)
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Advertisements

TaskPomo displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain information as outlined in Google's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Export and Import

TaskPomo provides features to export and import your data. When you use these features:

  • Export creates a JSON file containing your tasks and statistics
  • This file is saved to your device or shared through your device's sharing capabilities
  • We do not have access to exported data

Children's Privacy

TaskPomo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Reset the app to clear all data
